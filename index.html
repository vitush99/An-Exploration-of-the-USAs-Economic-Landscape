Exploring Economic Inequality in the U.S.

This project explores economic inequality in the United States, analyzing trends in income and wealth distribution across different states, demographics, and over time. Scroll down to discover insights and visualizations.

Introduction

Economic inequality in the United States has been a growing concern. By examining data from various sources, this project aims to uncover trends and disparities that shape the economic landscape. Through interactive visualizations, we aim to make these complex trends accessible and engaging.
